Section 3

Freelance Recruiter / Interviewer — Earnings Policy

Earnings are not "refunds" in the consumer sense. This section governs when platform-held earnings are disbursed or withheld, and when escrow amounts are returned to clients.

3.1 Mock Interview Earnings

Earnings for completed mock interviews are credited to the Interviewer's platform wallet within 48 hours of session completion, pending absence of a Candidate dispute. If a dispute is filed and upheld (see Section 5), earnings for that session may be withheld or partially reversed.

3.2 Managed Recruitment Earnings (Escrow)

Earnings for managed recruitment referrals are held in escrow and disbursed in tranches:

  • Tranche 1 (50%): Released within 7 business days of client acceptance of the candidate shortlist.
  • Tranche 2 (50%): Released on Day 91 of confirmed employment, contingent on no early-exit notification from the client.

If the placed candidate exits before Day 90, the Recruiter has 15 days to submit a replacement. If replacement is not submitted or accepted, Tranche 2 is returned to the client. The client may, at their discretion, waive replacement and release partial Tranche 2 payment.

3.3 On-Demand Interview Earnings

Fees for on-demand interviews conducted within a managed job drive are released within 7 business days of the client approving the interview report. Client review SLA is 48 hours. If the client does not respond within 48 hours, the interview is deemed accepted and fees are released automatically.

3.4 Withdrawal to Bank Account

Wallet balances of ₹500 or above may be withdrawn to the registered bank account or UPI handle. Withdrawals are processed within 5 business days. TDS at the applicable rate (currently 5% under Section 194M/194C as applicable) will be deducted prior to withdrawal where the annual payout threshold is met. TDS certificates (Form 16A) will be issued quarterly.
